multidim.covertree.CoverTree.make_edges¶

CoverTree.make_edges(min_distance=0.0, max_distance=-1.0)[source]¶

Iterate over the edges between the points of the underlying PointCloud, where min_distance < length <= max_distance.

Uses the CoverTree type-1 friends for efficiency. This is called by PointCloud.build_edges()

Parameters:
min_distance: float

Minimum length. (Default: 0.0) Inequality means no self-edges!

max_distance: float

Maximum length. (Default: -1.0, meaning 2*self._r0, for all edges)
Yields:
triples (a,b,r), where a,b are the indices of points, and r is the
distance.
